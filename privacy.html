Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kochar Dental Clinic. We are committed to protecting your personal health information and your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you provide through our website and during your visits to our clinic in Jabalpur.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that allows us to provide better dental care services, including:

  • Personal Identity: Name, age, and gender.
  • Contact Details: Phone number (+91 9713088897) and email address.
  • Health Data: Dental history and previous treatments.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used strictly for medical and administrative purposes:

  • To schedule and confirm appointments.
  • To maintain accurate dental records for treatment.
  • To contact you regarding follow-ups or dental emergencies.
  • To improve our clinic's services and website experience.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your sensitive health data. Access to patient records is strictly limited to authorized medical staff at Kochar Dental Clinic.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Data may only be shared with diagnostic labs or specialists if required for your specific dental treatment, and only with your explicit consent.
